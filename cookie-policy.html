Our Approach to Cookies

FindForce maintains a minimal cookie policy focused on essential functionality. We do not use tracking, advertising, or marketing cookies.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Authentication Cookie

  • Identifier: findforce_session
  • Purpose: Maintains user authentication state
  • Duration: Session-based or until logout
  • Required: Yes - necessary for service functionality

Analytics Cookies

PostHog Analytics

  • Identifiers: ph_[identifier], posthog
  • Purpose: Product usage analytics, website analytics, and crash reporting
  • Duration: 1 year
  • Required: No - can be disabled in extension settings
  • Data Collected: Feature usage, performance metrics, error logs, website visitor behavior

PostHog helps us understand how users interact with our extension and website to improve the product. You can opt out of this tracking in your extension settings.

Cookies We Do Not Use

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking cookies
  • Third-party marketing cookies
  • Behavioral tracking cookies
  • Cross-site tracking cookies

Managing Cookie Preferences

Analytics Opt-Out

To disable PostHog product analytics:

  1. Open the FindForce extension
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Toggle "Product Analytics" to off

This will stop all telemetry collection while maintaining core functionality.
