Cookie Policy - FindForce.io

Effective Date: August 26, 2025

Last Updated: August 26, 2025

Our Approach to Cookies

FindForce uses cookies for essential functionality and analytics. We distinguish between website analytics (requiring consent) and extension telemetry (opt-out available). We do not use tracking, advertising, or marketing cookies.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

Authentication Cookie

  • Identifier: findforce_session
  • Purpose: Maintains user authentication state
  • Duration: Session-based or until logout
  • Legal Basis: Necessary for service functionality
  • Consent Required: No

Analytics Cookies (Consent Required)

PostHog Website Analytics

  • Identifiers: ph_[identifier], posthog
  • Purpose: Website analytics, page views, user journey analysis
  • Duration: 1 year
  • Legal Basis: Consent
  • Consent Required: Yes - via cookie banner
  • Data Collected: Page views, session duration, referral sources, device information, geographic region

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website to improve user experience and content. You can manage your consent via the cookie banner or browser settings.

Extension Telemetry (Opt-out Available)

PostHog Extension Analytics

  • Storage: Chrome extension local storage (not browser cookies)
  • Purpose: Extension feature usage, performance monitoring, crash reporting
  • Duration: Until extension uninstall or opt-out
  • Legal Basis: Legitimate interest for service improvement
  • Consent Required: No - but opt-out available
  • Data Collected: Feature interactions, error logs, performance metrics

Extension analytics help us improve functionality and fix bugs. This is separate from website cookies and can be disabled in extension settings.

Cookies We Do Not Use

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking cookies
  • Third-party marketing cookies
  • Behavioral tracking cookies
  • Cross-site tracking cookies

Managing Your Preferences

Website Analytics Consent

To manage website analytics cookies:

  1. Use the cookie banner when first visiting our website
  2. Click "Cookie Preferences" in the footer to modify settings
  3. Enable or disable analytics tracking as desired

Your choice will be remembered for 12 months.

Extension Analytics Opt-Out

To disable extension telemetry:

  1. Open the FindForce extension
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Toggle "Product Analytics" to off

This will stop all extension telemetry while maintaining core functionality.

Complete Analytics Disable

To disable all PostHog analytics:

  1. Reject analytics cookies via the website banner
  2. Disable telemetry in extension settings
  3. Clear existing PostHog cookies in browser settings

Note that this will remove all data associated with PostHog analytics.

GDPR Compliance

For EU residents, we ensure:

  • Explicit Consent: Website analytics require clear consent via banner
  • Granular Control: Separate opt-out for extension analytics
  • Easy Withdrawal: Change preferences anytime
  • Minimal Data: No cross-site tracking or profiling
  • Data Subject Rights: Access, rectification, erasure available
